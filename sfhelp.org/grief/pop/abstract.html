Typical Invisible Losses 

      People able to form true social bonds inevitably experience them breaking, by chance or choice over time. We all become attached to physical and invisible things.

      The loss of unseen things can be just as painful as losing something tangible, and take as just long to fully accept. Our society often ignores abstract losses like these. Do your family members do that?
