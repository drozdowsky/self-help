About Wholistic Health

        In this Web site, "human health" means "the degree to which a person functions mentally, spiritually, physically, and psychologically, compared to how they're 'designed' to function." The adjective "wholistic" (or holistic) spans all four health domains. A toxic environment, family, belief, event, or relation-ship is one which hinders or reduces someone's who-listic health. Nurturing relates to filling current primary needs in each of these four domains.

        Premises: leaders in high-nurturance families and other groups are usually guided by their true Self, and strive to achieve and maintain the wholistic health of all group members and people they affect.
