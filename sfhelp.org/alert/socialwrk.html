Opportunities for Social Workers

Clinical, medical, and community social workers and their agen-cies and departments are dedicated to assisting a wide range of people and groups to improve their lives. They provide counsel, re-sources, and support for troubled persons, families, and communi-ties.

Professional social workers are on the front line with needy peo-ple, so they have a unique chance to alert others to the effects of the [wounds + unawareness] cycle and how to avoid them. The professionals who train, license, hire, evaluate, and support social workers in all venues have the same opportunity.

Because social workers form and refer to networks of human-service professionals, they have an unusual chance to alert these colleagues to personality subselves, false-self wounds, wound recovery, and these vital topics. They must first validate these concepts for themselves to gain motivation and credibility.
