Opportunities for Military Professionals

All military services depend on all personnel being mentally and physically healthy. Because family dysfunction is so common, it's likely that a significant percentage of typical military recruits and inductees are Grown Wounded Children (GWCs).

It would benefit individuals and military organizations to screen new and existing personnel for psychological wounds - and to offer recovery help for GWCs. It would also benefit service women and men and their families to provide basic education on wounds, recovery, and on functional organizations

By definition, people guided by their true Selves make consistently wise short and long-term decisions. Ideally, the top people in any military organization should be guided by their true Selves. Then they can help their subordinates free their own true Selves to lead.
