Requisites for Effective Communication

  • Your true Self steadily guiding your personality - even in conflicts. Without this, these other factors won't help much; and...

  • Stable mutual respect about you and any communication partner, regardless of age, knowledge, gender, role, authority, or other differences. And you need...

  • Clarity on your and feelings and primary needs; and clarity on your mutual human rights; and...

  • The ability to judge if someone is guided by a true Self or false self; and...

  • Compassion for significantly-wounded and/or unaware people; and...

  • Growing fluency in seven communication skills and six tools  and...

  • The ability to give clear, respectful feedback to people.

      self-improvement Lessons 1 and 2 show how you can get these requisites.
