Key Premises in this Web Site

All Break the Cycle! Web articles and guidebooks are based on the ideas below. If you disagree with these ideas, what do you believe?

● Adults and kids live in and join groups to fill a hierarchical range of personal needs. Nurturance means "filling needs."

● High-nurturance families and organizations display unique traits, so they can be judged to be "low nurturance" to "high nurturance."

● A group's nurturance level significantly affects the wholistic development and behavior of each group member.

● Any group's nurturance level depends on who guides the leaders' person- alities and their awareness, priorities, and leadership skills.

● Typical unaware survivors of low-nurturance childhoods (a) endure signifi- cant psychological wounds (b) repeatedly choose wounded partners and asso- ciates, (c) often choose human-service occupations, (d) unconsciously seek or create low-nurturance workplaces; and (e) pass on the [wounds + unawareness] cycle; and...

● Typical wounded adults and kids will repress, justify, or deny that these premises (a) apply to them and toxically affect their (b) wholistic health and longevity and (c) relationships, success, and contentment.
