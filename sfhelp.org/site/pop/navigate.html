How to Navigate This Site

This Web site can be entered thru several hundred individual articles and video clips. New visitors can feel confused about how to find their way around. To orient yourself quickly and navigate effectively...

1) Read the site intro and premises.

2) Read the course outline without following any links. Then follow the link to the Lesson 1 study guide, print it, and check off each "assignment" as you complete it.

3) Do the same with each of the next six lessons, in order.

4) Site pages contain many hyperlinks to informational popups and other full pages. To avoid getting lost, don't start reading these full pages until you finish the current page. DO read and close the popups.

5) Note that the navigation bars at the top and bottom of every full Web page provides links to (a) the course outline, (b) the current study guide, (c) a link-index of all articles and worksheets in that lesson, and (d) a site-search form.
