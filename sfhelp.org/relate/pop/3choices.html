Make 3 Wise Commitment Choices

Premise - Over 50% of American unions end in psycho-logical or legal divorce. That starts in courtship when wounded, unaware, needy partners commit to the wrong people (each other), for the wrong reasons, at the wrong time (before they know how to reduce their wounds, communicate and grieve effectively, and maintain a mu-tually-satisfying relationship).

      Couples considering co-commitment can guard themselves and their descendents against making un-wise courtship decisions by patiently studying at least Lessons 1 thru 4 in this non-profit Web site. If either mate has prior kids, both partners and their relatives should study Lessons 1 thru 7.
