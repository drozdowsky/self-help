Evolve a Co-parenting Team

       Families which include kids are responsible for the complex job of nurturing - filling adults and kids' needs well for two decades. Many people feel "it takes a village to raise a child (well)" - i.e. it takes the patient wisdom of many adults to prepare a child for successful independent life. Forming and maintaining voluntary parenting cooperation among family adults can be challenging - specially in complex divorcing families and stepfamilies.

      Family adults are most likely to form a cooperative team if each of them (a) is guided by their true Self, and (b) knows effective communication skills, and (c) has all the requisites for healthy relationships. See Lessons 1-4 in this site. /  more detail
