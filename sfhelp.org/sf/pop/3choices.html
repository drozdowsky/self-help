Make 3 Wise Commitment Choices

Premise - It's widely estimated that over 50% of U.S.  stepfamily unions end in psychological or legal divorce. That starts in courtship when wounded, unaware, needy partners commit to the wrong people (partner + other co-parent/s + stepkids), for the wrong reasons, at the wrong time (before they know how to reduce their wounds, communicate and grieve effectively, and maintain a mutually-satisfying relationship).

      Couples can guard themselves and their descen-dents against making unwise courtship decisions by patiently studying Lessons 1 thru 7 in this non-profit Web site before committing!.
