Alien Stepfamily Roles

Two of ~30 adjustment tasks that typical new stepfamily  members face are negotiating and stabilizing (a) many or all of these unfamiliar roles (family responsibilities), and (b) the rules of conduct that apply to each role:

 

stepmother

stepsister

stepdaughter

half-sister

step-niece

stepfather

stepbrother

stepson

half-brother

step-nephew

step-grandmother

step-grandfather

step-uncle

step-aunt

step-cousin

Note that these are role-titles, not persons!
