Six Vital Courtship Questions

        Studying Lessons 1 thru 7 in this Web site can prepare courting partners to answer these key questions wisely:

  • What needs am I trying to fill by committing?

  • Why should I commit to this person and their family?

  • Why  should I marry now? Are we all ready?

  • Bioparents: "If we can't find compromises, can I usually put our relationship before my kids' needs without excessive guilt?"
    Stepparents: "Am I convinced that my partner will want to put our relationship ahead of my stepkids' needs often enough for me? (Doing so protects everyone from divorce, long-term).

  • What are my other options now?

  • Is my true Self answering these questions? If not, your answers may be distorted
