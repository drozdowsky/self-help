Satisfactions - the Lesson-2 Guidebook

All human behavior is an attempt to satisfy current needs - (discomforts). All animals evolve ways of communicating to fill their social needs. Based on 40 years' research, this practical guidebook offers (a) communication basics and (b) seven powerful skills that your parents probably didn't teach you.
      If you can't name the skills, you're probably not using them - or satisfying your needs as often as you could. Mo-deling and teaching these basics and skills to the young people in your life is a priceless life-long gift!
