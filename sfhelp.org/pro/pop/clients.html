Six Types of Clinical Family Client

This clinical model focuses on five phases of the family life cycle. Each phase has unique dynamics and needs. The "/" in re/wedded and re/divorcing notes it may be a stepparent's first union. Key clinical focuses and interventions vary by client type. Typical client adults of each type are unaware of their primary stressors.
